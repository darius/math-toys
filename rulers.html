Click anywhere on a ruler to create a new point. On an existing point: click to select or deselect it. Drag it to move it around.

To add: select a point a, then slide the top ruler to move the 0 mark to a point b. The sum a+b appears.

To multiply: select a point a, then stretch the bottom ruler (by dragging it) to move the 1 mark to a point b. The product ab appears.
