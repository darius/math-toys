Click anywhere on the canvas to create a new point. On an existing point: click to select or deselect it. Drag it to move it around. (But you can't drag the constants 0 and 1.)

To add: select a point a, then drag from 0 to a point b. The sum a+b appears.

To multiply: select a point a, then drag from 1 to a point b. The product ab appears.
